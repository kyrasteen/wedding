together

We met in an economics class at Humboldt State University. Kyra was studying art and business and Christopher was studying biology and botany. After quite some time, and one coffee date, Kyra asked Chris to go skiing, (and had secretly already rented his gear). After being rained out of the slushy snow, we had a wonderful dinner together. We have been together for over six lovely years. Throughout the rest of our time in northern California, we lived two blocks apart, and shared many memorable times with our friends and roomates. You never know when enrolling in Economics 104 could change your life.

proposal

Christopher has never been one to be passionate about Christmas decorations. I figured he was just excited to use his saw when he insisted we get a fir tree for our tiny apartment. We drove across a snowy pass to reach forest service land, with cross-country skiis in tow! Christopher had been designing the engagement ring since the past spring with my jewelry friend and mentor Jennifer. I had no idea. He had also told everyone he could, that he was proposing that day and even organized a celebration that very evening. Confidence. Truddging through fluffy snow, we considered trees with critical eyes. Once we agreed on a cute little fir, Christopher knelt to the base of the tree with his saw. In one fell swoop a box was in his hand enclosing the most unique ring, and a question that left me speechless....for a minute!
